Primary rainy season in the city is from June to September when Bengaluru receives rain from South-West Monsoon, and secondary rainy season is from November to December when it receives rain from North-East Monsoon. Here is the raw data for rainfall in 100 years, from 1901 to 2000.

Situated at an altitude of 949 meters above sea level, Bangalore experiences a pleasant weather throughout the year because of its high elevation. The city experiences distinct wet and dry spells with the occasional heat waves during the summer and the cool winter breeze.
